Our Story

At Wild Bean we started with a simple idea: roast delicious coffee and share it with our neighborhood. Founded in 2017, we roast small batches weekly and partner with farmers to source direct-trade beans.

We believe in transparency, sustainable sourcing, and creating a welcoming space for everyone—whether you need a morning pick-me-up or a place to focus for a few hours.

Mission & Timeline

2017 — Humble Beginnings

Opened our first shop in a renovated bakery. First roast — 30 kg.

2019 — Roastery

Moved production to a small roastery; began offering wholesale to local cafes.

2022 — Community Focus

Launched community events: brew workshops, coffee tastings, and open mic nights.
